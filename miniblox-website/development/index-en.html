Discover the growth and evolution of Miniblox.io

2022/4/27

BlockCraft now allows account creation, and a new minigame "One in the Quiver" has been added.

2022/6/5

Players can now choose skins and purchase ranks (PRO, LEGEND).

Capes have been added, currently only available for players with the LEGEND rank.

2022/7/11

New command released: Chunk Claiming.

Use the /showclaims command to view a map of all claimed chunks around you.

Then use the /claim command to claim the chunk you are standing in. If the chunk is already claimed, you cannot claim it again.

To unclaim a chunk, use /unclaim or /unclaimall to remove all your claimed chunks.

Hopefully this will prevent further griefing.

2022/8/8

Added various new blocks, including most redstone components, pistons, furnaces, signs, and chests.

Survival improvements: Food can now be eaten and farmed.

Fixed major bugs related to Chunk Claiming.

2022/9/12

New parkour server released!

Can you complete all 100 levels?

2022/10/12

New minigame SPLEEF added with three unique modes. Win by surviving the longest as the last player alive.

Normal mode: Use your golden shovel to break blocks and gain snowballs to throw.

Bow mode: Use your bow to shoot arrows and break blocks. Left-click for a massive arrow barrage.

Run mode: Keep running, as blocks beneath you disappear!

After each round, use /vote to select the next game mode.

2022/10/18

Game renamed to Miniblox to better reflect the game's core concept.

This does not affect gameplay; players can continue playing servers as usual.

Upcoming weeks will mainly involve UI changes.

2022/12/15

New lobby system replaces the server selection menu. Press "Play" in the title screen to enter the lobby; click on a sign to join a server automatically.

EggWars minigame added, first map: Invasion. Collect resources to protect your egg, purchase blocks, tools, and weapons, build to opponents' bases, destroy their eggs, and the last team standing wins.

Player leveling system: Earn XP by playing on servers, from playtime or kills; display your level in chat.

Leaderboards are back and updated per server type: PvP servers track kill streaks, Creative servers show a map of claimed chunks nearby.

Creative servers get a trust system, allowing friends to build on your claimed chunks. Commands: /trust <player>, /untrust <player>, /trustlist.

3x XP boost event this week!

Shop now allows gifting ranks to players as Christmas presents.

Happy winter holidays!
